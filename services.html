Services

Mixing and Mastering

Mixing and mastering, both which pertain to the technical practice of achieving higher quality sound, are also forms of art in themselves. If you like what I do and how I do it, feel free to hit me up for more info and/or to request a quote for either or both services :)

Turnaround time:

3-7 days

What will be required:

For Mixing:

  • Exported Stems in WAV or AIFF format
  • Each stem bounced from beginning to end of duration of the full project
  • Each track titled and organized by the category they belong to (ex: kick/drums, snare/claps, vocals, fx, bass, sub, instruments, synth 1, synth 2, etc...)
  • Between - 3 db and - 6 db of headroom
  • Rough mix/demo of your work

For Mastering:

  • Stereo file in WAV or AIFF format
  • Between -3 db and - 6 db of headroom

What will be delivered:

  • Up to 3 revisions
  • Final mix/master in WAV and MP3 format (NOTE: snippets of the work/progress will be sent during the process. The full version will only be delivered after guaranteed satisfaction and final payment have been received.)

Payment:

Via paypal only.

Pay half upfront, pay the rest ONLY if and when you are happy with the final results. As an artist myself, I know what it's like to put so much time, effort, and care into a project, so I will always do my best to cater towards your vision while using my own. A happy client is a happy service provider! :)
